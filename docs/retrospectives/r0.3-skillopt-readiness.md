## Findings

### Finding 1

Classification: `non-blocking`

Observed behavior:

R0.3 completed before the repository had a standardized Claude handoff,
automated test entry points, and a finalized SkillOpt readiness artifact.

Expected behavior:

Future non-trivial R0.X milestones should use one durable Claude handoff,
evaluate the full test matrix, and complete SkillOpt readiness before final
Claude review.

Instruction weakness:

The workflow requirements were previously distributed across conversation
context instead of being executable from `AGENTS.md` and repository tools.

Evidence:

The current branch adds the handoff template/generator, Vitest and Playwright
entry points, and the SkillOpt readiness template/generator.

### Finding 2

Classification: `deferred`

Observed behavior:

Human listening QA was not completed before the R0.3 merge. The human
explicitly accepted that listening-only uncertainty as a post-merge follow-up.

Expected behavior:

Audio milestones should record human listening evidence before claiming audio
acceptance.

Instruction weakness:

The requirement now exists clearly in `AGENTS.md`; no optimization run is
needed to discover or restate it.

Evidence:

`docs/R0.3_CLOSEOUT_AND_FORWARD_NOTES.md` records the waiver and the required
follow-up.
